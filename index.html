Our Services

Veneers

They are a layer of material laid over the tooth to mask unwanted colors and shapes of the teeth, improving the dental appearance and aesthetics of your smile, making possible that perfect smile, they are of 2 types: PREP and NO-PREP veneers.

Implantology

The dental implant is an artificial tooth root, a titanium screw, surgically placed at the jaw bone instead of a missing tooth or altered, can be used to replace one or more teeth integrating perfectly into the bone structure.

Orthodontics

It is a branch of dentistry specialized in preventing and correcting unsightly, improperly positioned teeth, helping to improve dental aesthetics, to protect teeth, gums and maxillary joint.

Odontotherapy

It deals with the diagnosis, prevention and treatment of carious lesions, dental caries is a lesion that destroys the structure of teeth in stages Incipient, they can be noticed and prevented after a regular check-up at the dentist.

Cosmetic dentistry

It refers to dental treatments that improve the appearance of teeth, gums and smile, veneers and teeth whitening are considered procedures cosmetics, our clinic offers a full range of cosmetic dental treatments.

Facial Aesthetics

Minimally invasive aesthetic treatments can eliminate fine expression lines and wrinkles formed over time. They improve skin texture And they can make you look 10 years younger as soon as you walk out the door of our office.
